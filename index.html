A Mosaic of Memory and Modernity

Pinwatta represents the unique blend of Sri Lankan coastal heritage and contemporary suburban living, creating a community that honors its past while embracing the future.

Surfing Heritage

Home to Sri Lanka's pioneering surfers since 1968, Pinwatta's coastline holds the legacy of the island's surf culture.

Sacred Architecture

The Rankoth Viharaya temple, founded in 1810, stands as a testament to centuries of spiritual and cultural continuity.

Community Spirit

A diverse, harmonious community where different cultures and generations come together in the spirit of unity.

Strategic Location

Perfectly positioned along Galle Road with convenient access to Panadura and Colombo, offering the best of both worlds.
