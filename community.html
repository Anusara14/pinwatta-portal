ENVIRONMENT

Nature's Embrace

Understanding Pinwatta's tropical climate, seasonal patterns, and environmental challenges that shape daily life.

Tropical Monsoon Climate

Pinwatta enjoys a warm, humid tropical monsoon climate characterized by distinct wet and dry seasons. The village experiences average temperatures between 25°C to 32°C throughout the year, with high humidity levels typical of coastal Sri Lanka.

Southwest Monsoon

May to September - Primary rainy season with heavy rainfall and strong coastal winds.

Inter-Monsoon

March to April & October to November - Transitional periods with moderate rainfall.

Northeast Monsoon

December to February - Drier season with occasional showers and pleasant temperatures.

Environmental Considerations

High Humidity

Year-round humidity levels of 70-85% typical of coastal tropical climate.

Heavy Rainfall

Monsoon seasons bring intense rainfall requiring proper drainage systems.

Flood Risks

Low-lying coastal areas face occasional flooding during extreme weather events.
